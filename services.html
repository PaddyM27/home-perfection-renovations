Service 01
From €38,000

Transform dead space into living space.

Most Dublin homes have an attic that's either storage or empty air. We convert it into a fully finished room — bedroom, home office, playroom, or whatever suits your family — without the mess, delay, or surprise costs you've come to expect from building work.

Attic conversions under 40m² don't require planning permission — we handle all structural engineering, Velux installation, and the Cert of Compliance that protects you if you sell.

  • Structural steel and joists
  • Velux windows and insulation
  • Staircase design and installation
  • Electrical, lighting, and heating
  • Timber or engineered flooring
  • Full painting to walls and ceiling

Not if your attic conversion is under 40m². Most standard semi-detached homes qualify. We'll confirm this at your initial consultation and handle the engineering sign-off on structural works.

Most standard attic conversions take 5–7 weeks from start to handover. This includes all structural, finishing, and decorating works — you don't need to hire separate trades.

We provide Certificates of Compliance for all structural works upon request. This is issued by our registered engineer and protects your home's value when selling.

A kitchen rebuilt around how you cook.

We design and install bespoke kitchens that work for your layout, your habits, and your budget. No catalogue kitchens. Every unit is specified to your space and fitted by our own joiners.

Painting and flooring are always included — you never hand a half-finished kitchen back to yourself.

  • Quartz or granite worktops
  • Splashback tiling
  • Appliance integration and electrics
  • Floor tiling or engineered timber
  • Full painting and decorating

Cabinet styles

Classic recessed panel doors in a painted or raw timber finish. Timeless, warm, and works in both period and contemporary homes.

Flat, smooth cabinet doors with a clean, minimal profile. Often paired with handleless rail or J-pull for a seamless look.

Integrated push-to-open or rail grip systems eliminate handles entirely. Ideal for very modern or minimalist spaces.

Solid timber doors painted to your chosen colour. Adds warmth and depth — particularly effective in period or Victorian houses.

Service 03
From €48,000

More space, perfectly built in.

Whether you want to extend outward or upward, we deliver the full package: structural engineering, build, insulation, glazing, tiling, painting, and flooring. One team, one contract.

Extension type

Uses the narrow side passage beside your house to create a wider ground floor. Common in Dublin terraced houses — typically adds 8–15m² without touching the rear garden.

Extends into the rear garden, typically creating an open-plan kitchen-diner. Under 40m² doesn't require planning — our most common project type.

Adds both ground and first floor space — typically 2 rooms per floor. Planning permission required. We manage the full application and engineering process.

  • Structural engineering and foundations
  • Glazing, roof lanterns, and bi-fold doors
  • Plumbing, heating, and electrical
  • Tiling, flooring, and joinery
  • Full painting and decorating

Under 40m²? Extensions under 40m² don't require planning permission in most Dublin residential zones. We'll confirm your eligibility at the first consultation — free of charge.
